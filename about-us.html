Who we are

We make investment easy

Romatun Company Limited, A Vibrant, Energetic, and Innovative private company in the United Republic of Tanzania with Registration number 141-703-161 under the Companies Act of 2002. We recognize that the Real Estate industry is a people's industry, ROMATUN are big enough to cope with, and small enough to care about integrity, fairness and professionalism service.

ROMATUN aims at building and maintaining long-term relationships with our customers. We make and keep the trust of every client we serve. Our areas of specialization are Valuation, property management, Land consultancy and Real Estate Agency services. From our inception in 2020, we have gone from strength to strength - expanding our services, entering new markets, agreeing partnerships with partner companies, forming alliances, and participating in private and public strategic projects. In general, establishing ourselves in the Real Estate Industry as a big player. We are affiliated with the following professional board:

  • Valuers Registration Board (VRB)
  • Board of Valuer Zanzibar (BVZ)
  • Association of Real Estate Professionals of Tanzania (AREPTA).

This designation recognizes excellence in Real Estate firms and is the result of having achieved the highest levels of performance, experience and financial stability in our industry. We value the continuing education of our Team and provide opportunities for our staff to grow and improve everyday.
